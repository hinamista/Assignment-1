About Hinami:


An aspiring Virtual Youtuber who not only streams but also makes covers. With no affiliation to any company, Hinami works independently to ensure the best quality content for her viewers.

In terms of lore, she is a siren who decided to become a Vtuber for the means of earning money. The Siren species are known to lure sailors to their deaths using their angelic voices and beauty and stealing their souls to feed their hunger. Wanting to rid herself of this stereotypical prejudice against her kind, Hinami swore never to use her voice to control and prey on humans.

However, in modern day society, it is difficult to survive especially when one is of the minority race amongst all the other humans. Hence, Hinami came across the idea of vtubing, a quick and easy method to earn money and provide for herself through superchats and donations while also creating and providing content for the audience. While she may still be using her voice in order to stream and sing, she's happy to be entertaining the people and making them happy as well while doing so.
